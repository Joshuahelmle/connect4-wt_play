Overview

Connect 4 is a two player, turnbased strategy game.
You and your opponent take turns filling up the game board with your tokens of your color.
Your Goal is to build up consecutive lines of tokens to ultimately get 4 tokens in a row and win the game.
These rows can be vertical, horizontal or even diagonal! But beware, your enemy can and will disrupt your plans.

Play a Turn

At the start of the Game each of you picks a color, you'll get 21 Tokens of that color to try and win the game with.
If it's your turn you choose a Column to put in your token, the token then falls down until it either reaches the edge of the board or another Token
After your token has fallen down your opponent's turn begins.

End of Game

The Game ends as soon there is a consecutive row of 4 Tokens in the same color. This can happen either horizontally

Or vertically

or even diagonally!

The Game ends in a draw if both players happen to run out of Tokens and all 42 Fields in the Board are filled without 4 of the same color in a row.
